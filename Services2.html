Deep Tissue Massage

Deep tissue massage therapy is similiar to Swedish massage, but deeper is beneficial in releasing chronic muscle tension. The focus is on the deepest layers of muscle tissue, tendons and fascia (the protective layer surrounding muscles, bones and joints). The focus is in realigning deeper layers of muscles and connective tissue. It's espedcially helpful for chronic aches and pains and contracted areas such as stiff neck, upper back, low back pain. leg muscle tightness, and sore shoulders.
